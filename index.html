About Me

I was born in Rochester, New York, and I currently live in Longwood, Florida. I got my Bachelor's of Fine Arts with a focus on painting in 2016 from the University of Florida.

My favorite animals are harbor seals, and elephant seals. Elephant seal pups, once weaned, are called weaners. Sometimes male weaners will try to continue suckling from other females who will still allow them to. They will balloon to huge sizes, earning them the scientific title of super weaners.
